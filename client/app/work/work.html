Slackbot Reminders - Visit Site

Description

A weekend project to help an agile team communicate effectively. This application allows users of slack to set up one-time or repeating reminders as slackbot, once slackbot integration is enabled on your team's site. The initial use case was to notify team members of the daily scrum call. You can send reminders to channels or individual users.
